Game description


This is Sharkie.
You are playing the character of a shark who has to collect coins and poison bottles
to attack the endboss. Every coin and every poison bottle is equal to one bubble attack.
You can also hit the enemies with bubble attacks, so they die.
The poison bottles are just useable for the endboss.
Enemies and the endboss can also be hit with fin slaps.
If you want to know what keys you need to press for the attacks go to the instructions.
Have fun playing!
